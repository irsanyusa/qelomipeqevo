A Nigerian lady identified as Oladimeji Oluwatosin has called out her husband for lying about his financial status before they got married.

According to Oluwatosin, she and her husband named Emmanuel Egba got married in February 2022 after dating for six months.

How it happened

Narrating her story in a post shared on Instagram by @instablog9ja, Oluwatosin said she got to Emmanuel's house after the wedding and discovered to her shock that he could not even feed her.
