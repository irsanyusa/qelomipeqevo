Richard Branson (Business Magnate) - On This Day

Profession: Business Magnate

Biography: Best known as the founder of the Virgin Group, English entrepreneur Richard Branson has an estimated worth of $5.2 billion (Forbes 2016).

Branson began his business career young opening music stores in 1971, which he grew into the Virgin Megastore chain. Branson then moved into the recording business starting Virgin Records in 1973.

In 1984 Branson moved into the aeronautic industry starting Virgin Atlantic Airways, then Virgin Publishing in 1991, Virgin Radio in 1993 and Virgin Mobile in 1999. Post 2000, Branson's empire has only expanded with Virgin Healthcare, a Formula One racing team and the spaceline venture Virgin Galactic in 2004.

Branson is also known for his pursuit of distance and speed records, recording the fastest crossing of the Atlantic in 1986 but being just beaten in his attempt to circumnavigate the globe in a balloon in 1998.

Born: July 18, 1950
Birthplace: London, England
