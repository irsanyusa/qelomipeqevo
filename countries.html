Belize History Timeline - Important Dates &amp; People

Events in Belize History

  • 1798-09-03 Battle of St. George's Caye: Week long battle begins between the Spanish Empire and Great Britain off the coast of Belize
  • 1798-09-10 British Honduras beats Spain in battle of St George
  • 1961-10-31 Hurricane Hattie kills 400 in British Honduras
  • 1975-11-05 British government sends troops to Belize
  • 1981-09-21 Belize (British Honduras) gains independence from UK
  • 2005-01-21 In Belmopan, Belize, the unrest over the government's new taxes erupts into riots

Event of Interest

2022-03-27 Duke and Duchess of Cambridge end an eight-day tour of Belize, Jamaica and the Bahamas that highlighted issues of decolonization and calls for slave reparations [1]


Famous People from Belize

  • 1897-02-22 Bob Ysaguirre, American jazz double bass and tuba player (Don Redman Orchestra), born in Belize, British Honduras (now Belize City, Belize) (d. 1982)
  • 1944-03-19 Said Musa, Prime Minister of Belize
  • 1958-04-10 Errollyn Wallen, British contemporary classical composer (Mighty River; Carbon 12), born in Belize, British Honduras
