Updated: December 26, 2017 10:34 PM [ET] | Originally published: December 26, 2017 9:16 PM EST; An estimated 40,000 people in Cleveland, Ohio were without power Tuesday night, as temperatures dipped to a frigid 13 degrees Fahrenheit (-11°C). Power was knocked out across the city’s west side, including Old Brooklyn, Tremont, Battery Park and the Flats neighborhoods, local radio station wykc reports.
