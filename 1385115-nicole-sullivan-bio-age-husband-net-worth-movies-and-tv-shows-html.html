Nicole Sullivan bio: age, husband, net worth, movies and TV shows

Nicole Sullivan is a well-recognised comedy actress who is famous due to the countless roles she has played in TV shows. Her career path and personal life are quite exciting to learn about, so you can get to know her better through her comprehensive biography.

Nicole Sullivan biography

The celebrity's birthplace is Manhattan, New York, United States. Her mother was a businesswoman, while her father was a Democratic politician who represented their home district in the New York State Assembly.

From a young age, she took dancing classes and performed in Broadway productions as a part of the children's theatre. In high school, she was very athletic - she played soccer and basketball.

Nicole became a theatre major at Northwestern University after her high school graduation. She was an excellent student and graduated with honours, before moving on to start her career on television.

One of her very first prominent works was being a part of the MADtv cast. On this sketch comedy series, she portrayed numerous personalities, the most famous of which was Vancome Lady.

She gained prominence for playing the role of Holly Shumpert on the sitcom "The King of Queens". A few of her other well-known roles are in "Rita Rocks", "Cougar Town", "Let It Shine", "Wendell and Vinnie", and "Black-ish".

Apart from being a talented actress, she is also a gifted voice artist. Some of her voice roles include Mira Nova in "Buzz Lightyear of Star Command", Shego in "Kim Possible", a few recurring roles in "Family Guy", Franny Robinson in "Meet the Robinsons", and Marlene in "The Penguins of Madagascar".
