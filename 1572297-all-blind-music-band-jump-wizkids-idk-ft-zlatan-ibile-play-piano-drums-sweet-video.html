All-Blind Music Band Jumps on Wizkids IDK Ft Zlatan Ibile, Play Piano, Drums in Sweet Video

  • Wizkid's recently released song IDK featuring Zlatan Ibile has continued to trend days after it was released
  • Bethesda School of the Blind recently jumped on the new song as they shared a video of an all-blind band vibing to IDK
  • The video, which has since gone viral, has caught attention on social media as many applauded the blind band
  • Bethesda School in a chat with Legit.ng revealed their all-blind band were able to remake Wizkid's song in two days

Days after Nigerian music star Ayo Balogun 'Wizkid' dropped IDK, the new song featuring Zlatan Ibile has continued to gain attention.

Legit.ng recalls reporting that Wizkid revealed he made the song days after he lost his mother, Mrs Jane Dolapo, as he described life as meaningless.

It took two days to record - Bethesda School

Bethesda School recorded a remake of Wizkid's IDK featuring Zlatan Ibile in two days.

The school said:

"We selected the song a week after the EP came out, it took us 2 days to record the song and perform it."

Man makes a vow after Wizkid touched him

Legit.ng recalls reporting about a Nigerian man who had the rare opportunity to meet Wizkid.

In a reaction, the man vowed never to take off his jacket after one of his biggest music idols touched it.

In a viral clip shared online by the man, he revealed he bumped into the Afrobeat star at a Lagos nightclub. Wizkid and the young man in the viral clip also shared a brief moment.
