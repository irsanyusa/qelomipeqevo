The Truth About Ariana Grande, Ethan Slater's Relationship, Did They See Each Other While Married?

Ariana Grande and Ethan Slater’s alleged romance has been the talk of the town for quite some time now. Since rumors and speculation began swirling about their “affair,” fans have been wondering about the couple’s relationship timeline. A source close to the couple has spoken out against claims of infidelity, asserting that they only started seeing each other after both had separated from their respective partners.

Ariana Grande, Ethan Slater Started Dating After Separating From Their Previous Partners

The story began back in July when Grande’s divorce from her husband of two years, Dalton Gomez, was announced just three days before reports of her involvement with Slater started circulating. Notably, Slater also recently ended his four-year marriage to Lilly Jay. The timing certainly raised eyebrows and led to a ton of gossip.

At the time, one insider even claimed that their alleged affair was an open secret on the set of the Wicked movie. According to this source, their actions on set were far from discreet. With allegations that they were frequently seen getting close to each other while Slater was still supposedly married to Jay.

Moreover, it was suggested that the two couples went on several double dates in London while filming was taking place. Making the situation even more complicated.

Adding fuel to the fire, there were reports that Grande had met Slater and Jay’s newborn baby during their time in London. She apparently expressed her desire to have children in the future during conversations with Jay. These claims painted a picture of a tangled web of emotions and relationships.

A Close Friend Of The Couple Said The Situation Has Been Blown Out of Proportion

Grande and Slater’s relationship has faced intense scrutiny, but numerous sources have come forward to refute claims of an affair. Additionally, they clarified that their romance began after they had separated from their respective spouses.

A friend of the couple recently stated that the situation had been blown out of proportion. They also said that the reality is much different from what has been portrayed in the media. They emphasized that Grande and Slater want their relationship to be private, away from the prying eyes of the public.

Acknowledging the confusion surrounding the timeline, the source affirmed that Grande and Slater only started seeing each other after both had respectfully separated from their partners. It’s a statement that aligns with previous reports that both parties are doing their best to maintain a balance between their public personas and their private lives while respecting everyone involved.

Grande and Slater are set to star in the highly anticipated film adaptation of the hit Broadway musical Wicked. With Grande playing Glinda and Slater portraying Boq. The movie’s production commenced in the UK in 2022, with a scheduled release date in November 2024. Though it remains uncertain if the SAG-AFTRA strike will affect its premiere.
