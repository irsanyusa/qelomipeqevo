Heres How Meghan Trainor First Met Her Husband Daryl Sabara

Photo by Paul Archuleta/FilmMagic

Reading Time: 2 minutes

We’ve gotten some crumbs of Meghan Trainor and Daryl Sabara’s first time meeting, but it wouldn’t hurt to hear it again from the pop star herself. In her recent appearance in The Drew Barrymore Show, Trainor explains how she sparked romance with her now husband.

Meghan Trainor Fangirled Over Daryl Sabara Before

Apparently, the “Made You Look” singer first encountered Sabara when she was still starting in Hollywood. Way back, Trainor attended a songwriter party that Sabara coincidentally crashed with his friends. Recalling this moment, Trainor said that she fangirled heavily when the moment she saw the Spy Kids actor.

“I went like right to him I was ‘Are you Spy Kids?’ and he was like ‘Yeah’ and it was really nice,” she said.

According to the singer, approaching Sabara was unusual for her because she normally doesn’t say anything as a fangirl. Little did she know, that cute encounter was the beginning of her love story.

“Two years later, I became a famous pop star and I was friends with Chloe Moretz,” she said. “I was like ‘I want a nice guy, I just I’m sick of all these mean guys I want a nice guy.'”

Acting matchmaker and doing it successfully, Chloe Moretz invited Trainor and Sabara for a double date, and the love magic just happened. Since then, the two have “never been apart.”

She Talks About Motherhood, Body Positivity

A couple of years after they got married, Trainor and Sabara welcomed their first born, Riley. This milestone in Trainor’s life inspired her to create more music amid motherly responsibilities.

“Luckily, I produce and record my vocals myself, so I do a lot of work at my house,” she said. “I built a studio in my basement and then I told my co-writers like we can start at 10 a.m. but I’m wrapped by 6 p.m. because I’m there for a bath time and bedtime.”

Trainor’s creative process has much to do with voicing out what other women are scared to speak about. Reflecting on her smash hit “Me Too,” the pop star said that she composed it while feeling unconfident. Nevertheless, she proceeded to do so because she needs to be comforted by the lyrics.

“I’m working everyday, I’m trying to better myself and trying to love myself more,” she said. “That’s what these songs are about,” she said.

Now, she intends to empower as a mom. Trainor recently released her newest studio album, Takin’ It Back, rediscovering self-love and maturity. Fans have playfully been vibing with this new collection.
