SZAs Instagram account reveals Nigerian name

Grammy-nominated with her debut album, Ctrl, SZA was born Solana Imani Rowe. But over the past few days, her Instagram page, SZA now carries a Nigerian name, ‘Amarachi Chinonso.’

Both names are of Igbo origin. ‘Amarachi’ means ‘God’s grace’ while Chinonso means, ‘God is close.’
