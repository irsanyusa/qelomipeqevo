Stephen tWitch Boss Archives - Talent Recap

BIO: Stephen “tWitch” Boss is known for being the longrunning DJ on The Ellen Degeneres Show. Outside of the talk show, he acts as host of both Clash of The Cover Bands and Real Dirty Dancing. He joined the judging panel for So You Think You Can Dance for the show’s revival.

Boss rose to fame on the series, The Wade Robson Project and Star Search in 2003. He placed third in The Wade Robson Project and later won second place in Star Search. He then competed on So You Think You Can Dance in 2008. He ended the season as runner up to Joshua Allen. He then moved on to his position on The Ellen Degeneres Show in 2014. Along with the talk show, Boss has been featured in many films such as Magic Mike XXL, the 2016 adaptation of Ghostbusters, and more.

In 2013, Boss married his current wife, So You Think You Can Dance alum Allison Holker. At the time of their marriage, Boss also adopted Holker’s daughter, Weslie Renae Boss. The couple has three children, Weslie Renae Boss, Maddox Laurel Boss, and Zaia Boss.
