Ciara Stuns On Bride Magazine Cover In A Dream Wedding Gown!

Ciara graces the new cover in a long-sleeve embroidered Chantilly lace Reem Acra gown, accessorized with her very own blinding bridal bling and bracelet. A simply-chic bouquet of cream garden roses, crafted by Miho Kosuda, plus soft, natural makeup by Urban Decay and effortless blonde waves tied the entire aisle-ready look together.

Inside the issue, Ciara dishes all about Future, motherhood, and, of course, her wedding plans. The singer admitted she "loved" the classic dress she rocks on the cover, which is surprising since she's known for her daring red carpet looks.
