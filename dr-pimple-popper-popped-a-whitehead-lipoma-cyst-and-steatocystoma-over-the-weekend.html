Dr. Pimple Popper Popped A Whitehead, Lipoma, Cyst, And Steatocystoma Over The Weekend

Dr. P kicked off her pop-a-thon with a video that focused on a lipoma. L-I-P-O-M-A and Lipoma was its name-oh! Sing a long and catch the whole removal on #YouTube now! #bingo#lipoma #drpimplepopper, she captioned the video.

The doctor isnt messing around with this one: The video cuts straight to a shot of a man with a massive lipoma (a benign tumor made of fatty tissue, JIC youre not up to date on your derm lingo) cut open on the side of his face.

She can be seen rummaging around in there, pulling out chunks of fatty tissue with tweezers as the patient is actually chuckling and talking about baseball. At one point, he says that he feels nothing, which is great, because looking at the whole thing is putting me in pain. The doctor finally pulls out a big, red ball of gunk and that is that.

The next video features a grody-looking whitehead thats apparently on someones chest, according to the caption. Dr. Pimple Popper says it looks like an eye, and theres no arguing that. In the video, the doctor slices right into the center before squeezing the heck out of it. Nasty yellowish gunk slowly globs out, and she pulls it out using tweezers and scissors.

On to the next one! This video features a steatocystoma (a growth that begins in the skin's sebaceous glands, which normally produce oil) in what appears to be the patients armpit. And-vomit alert-the doctor actually has the patient squeeze it herself after she slices open the top. Tons of yellowish goo squirts out and the patient can be heard saying, Goodbye!

And finally, the doctor shares a video of what she calls a perfect Sunday cyst punch. Its not oozing or anything, she can be heard saying, as you see a closeup of a mans face. The cyst is barely noticeable but the doctor reaches for her trusty punch tool and pretty soon you see why. She starts squeezing and a nasty column of blood-coated goo comes out like a worm. Thats a lot stuck in there, she says, before going in for more.

Yeah, maybe dont eat while watching all of this .
