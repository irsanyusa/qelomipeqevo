Zodiac signs stuck in loveless relationships

It can be a conscious choice, and sometimes it is a continuation of the relationship, even though the feeling has already died out.

Some are very sensitive and emotional, while others are painfully practical. They have one thing in common - in many cases, their relationship is just a fiction, in which there is no longer any feeling.

1. Cancer

Cancers are very emotional, and stubborn. They have a hard time admitting they've made a mistake, and they treat a breakup as a personal failure.

Instead, they prefer to pretend that everything is fine and stay in a bad relationship. They value stability and order, so even if they're not happy, they won't let their peace be destroyed, especially if they have children.

2. Virgo

ADVERTISEMENT

In a relationship, they are looking primarily for a soul mate, not necessarily love.

3. Libra

Libras are consistent and persistent. If they decide to start a relationship, they will try to maintain it despite the difficulties. Instead of breaking up, they prefer to stay in a failed relationship so as not to hurt their partner or harm their children.

4. Capricorn

ADVERTISEMENT

Capricorns are pragmatic and practical. In a relationship, they value comfort and peace, not necessarily love. They prefer to focus on work and developing their business, and the partner simply has to be there and make a good impression.

5. Pisces

Pisces are dreamers and idealists who can't end a relationship, even if there's no love in it. They'd rather suffer than hurt someone and leave. Unfortunately, they sometimes confuse love with sympathy, lust, or even pity or fear.

Remember that this is just a horoscope and treat it as a curiosity. A person's character and their relationships depend on many different factors, not just the zodiac sign. Above all, it is important that each relationship is based on respect, understanding and mutual support.
