42 photos that show how Reese Witherspoon's style has evolved over the years

Witherspoon often gravitates toward pieces that seem to be influenced by whatever project she's working on; for example, she wore pink pieces while promoting the " Legally Blonde " franchise, and an ethereal gown to the premiere of"Just Like Heaven."

Work-inspired ensembles aside, the actor has a distinct personal fashion aesthetic that lives at the intersection of vintage and modern . From cardigans and combat boots to glamorous gowns and heels, keep scrolling to see how Witherspoon's style has changed since 1996.
