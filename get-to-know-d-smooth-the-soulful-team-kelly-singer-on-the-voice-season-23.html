Get to Know D. Smooth, The Soulful Team Kelly Singer On 'The Voice' Season 23

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice

Casey Durkin/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

D. Smooth, whose real name is David Mitchell, is an American singer and songwriter who gained recognition participating in Season 23 of NBC’s popular singing competition show, The Voice. Hailing from Birmingham, Alabama, D. Smooth captured the attention of the judges and viewers with his remarkable vocal talent and captivating performances.

Who is David “D. Smooth” Mitchell?

Born on September 28, 1997, in Alabama, D. Smooth is currently 25 years old. He completed his education at Minor High School in Alabama, graduating in 2016. From a young age, D. Smooth demonstrated a passion for music, although he initially pursued sports during his high school years.

D. Smooth’s musical journey took a significant turn when he made his debut in the touring musical theater production titled “Grown Man Issues” in the preceding year. The experience allowed him to showcase his abilities as a performer and strengthen his connection with music.

In 2023, D. Smooth’s career soared to new heights when he auditioned for The Voice Season 23. His captivating rendition of Ed Sheeran’s hit song “Perfect” during the Blind Auditions caught the attention of both Kelly Clarkson and Niall Horan, leading to a two-chair turn. After careful consideration, D. Smooth made the decision to join Team Kelly, embracing the guidance and mentorship of the talented coach.

As a singer, D. Smooth possesses a versatile and soulful voice that resonates with audiences. His musical style combines elements of pop, R&B, and soul, creating a unique and compelling sound that sets him apart.

The Voice Featured His Best Performances

The Voice Season 23 promises to be an exciting chapter in D. Smooth’s career, as he competes alongside other exceptional talents from around the country. The show features an esteemed panel of coaches, including Clarkson, Niall Horan, Chance The Rapper, and the iconic Blake Shelton, who will be bidding farewell after this season. The contestants will battle it out through various rounds, aiming to impress the coaches and secure their place in the finals.

Fans and viewers can catch D. Smooth’s electrifying performances on The Voice, airing on NBC. Additionally, episodes can be streamed the following day on Peacock, offering an opportunity to witness the journey of this extraordinary singer.

As D. Smooth continues to captivate audiences with his talent, charisma, and dedication to music, his presence on The Voice Season 23 serves as a testament to his passion and relentless pursuit of his dreams. Keep an eye out for this rising star, as he aims to make his mark on the music industry and touch the hearts of listeners around the world.
