15 Celebs Who Stay Loyal to Their Romantic Type

Attraction often holds an air of mystery. Scientists have found that people usually have a specific kind of person they prefer to date. Even if they want to try dating someone different, like after a breakup, many still end up choosing partners who are alike in some ways. And the celebrities in our list actually prove this.

Brad Pitt

After a long-time relationship with Angelina Jolie Brad Pitt constantly make headlines with the rumors of him dating someone. Coincidence or not, all his romantic interests afterwards remind Jolie of their appearance, take for example, Nicole Poturalski (in the middle) or Ines de Ramon whom Pitt is dating right now.

Lili Reinhart

Lili Reinhart has recently confirmed her relationship with actor Jack Martin (on the right) in a public manner. However, observant fans have noticed an intriguing similarity — he bears a striking resemblance to her former boyfriend, Cole Sprouse (left). Before Jack even parodied Sprouse’s Call Her Daddy interview in a skit where he pretended to be the “third Sprouse brother.”

Katy Perry

Katy Perry and Orlando Bloom are engaged and enjoying parenthood as they raise their daughter together. But if we take a look at Katy’s ex-boyfriend John Myer one may assume that the singer has a particular romantic type.

Leonardo DiCaprio

ASSOCIATED PRESS/East News, Evan Agostini/Invision/AP/East News, Rich Fury/Invision/AP/East News, Laurent VU/SIPA/SIPA/East News

Gisele Bündchen, Amber Valletta, Toni Garrn, Erin Heatherton, — what’s the common trend among these women? They are all models adorned with blonde hair who have all been romantically involved with Leonardo DiCaprio.

Khloé Kardashian

The ex-husband of Kardashian, the former LA Laker Lamar Odom (on the left), shares traits of height, athleticism, and court prowess, much like her ex-boyfriend Tristan Thompson (right). Kardashian herself has mentioned, “I have a type. I’ve never been with anybody but a black man.”

Adam Levine

MCMULLAN CO/SIPA/EAST NEWS, Evan Agostini/Invision/East News

The lead singer of Maroon 5 Adam Levine and coach on The Voice has a clear preference for supermodels, particularly those from Victoria’s Secret. His wife, Behati Prinsloo (on the right), and his former partner, Anne V (on the left), share striking similarities in this regard.

Nicolas Cage

Can Nguyen/Capital Pictures/EAST NEWS, Charles Sykes/Invision/East News

If we look at the last relationships of Nicolas Cage the same romantic pattern can be clearly noticed. Cage married Alice Kim (on the left) when she was 19 years old, whereas Cage was 40. Their marriage didn’t last for long and later Nicholas married Riko Shibata who may remind Alice and is also over 30 years younger than Cage.

Ellen DeGeneres

Mary Evans/AF Archive/Graham Whitby Boot./Mary Evans Picture Library/East News, John Shearer/Invision/AP/East News

The former partner of the talk show host Ellen DeGeneres, Anne Heche (on the left), bears a resemblance to Ellen’s current spouse, Portia de Rossi (right). Both are blonde women with blue eyes.

George Clooney

SamPayne@Broadimage/Broad Image/East News, Dee Cercone/Everett Collection/ East News, Fati Sadou/ABACAPRESS.COM/Abaca/East News

There’s no doubt in the all-encompassing charm of Amal Clooney, the woman who succeeded in getting the long-time bachelor, George to say “I do.” However, the previous girlfriends of Clooney like Stacy Keibler (in the middle) and Elisabetta Canalis (right) don’t look less charmer and even remind Amal in some way.

Charlie Sheen

Mary Evans/Allstar/Graham Whitby Boot/Mary Evans Picture Library/East News, face to face/Reporter / East News

The former spouse of the Two and a Half Men star Charlie Sheen, Denise Richards (left), bears a curious resemblance to his other former wife, Brooke Mueller (right): both are blonde and model-looking women.

Ryan Reynolds

Ryan Reynolds’s past relationship with Scarlett Johansson and his current marriage with Blake Lively reinforce the notion that Reynolds is inclined not only towards stunning actresses but particularly towards fair-haired blondes with figures reminiscent of Marilyn Monroe’s.

Ethan Hawke

Columbia Pictures/Courtesy Everett Collection/Everett Collection/ East News, Evan Agostini/Invision/East News

Ethan Hawke’s initial spouse, Uma Thurman (on the left), and his present wife, Ryan Hawke (on the right), both possess similar traits of being tall and blonde, showcasing a somewhat uncanny resemblance.

Jennifer Lawrence

Gaz Shirley, PacificCoastNews.com/EAST NEWS, PHOTOlink/Courtesy Everett Collection/East News

Throughout her career, Jennifer Lawrence’s romantic associations have centered around Nicholas Hoult, a tall actor with striking ice-blue eyes (on the left). Following their separation, she found solace in a relationship with Chris Martin, the lead singer of Coldplay, who also possesses captivating blue eyes (on the right). Looking at the photos of both men it’s becoming evident, there’s a noticeable trend emerging here.

Emma Stone

Joel Ryan/Invision/AP/East News, White Label/East News

Emma Stone and Andrew Garfield were once one of Hollywood’s most beloved couples. However, Emma is now married to Dave McCary (on the right), who interestingly shares some similarities with Andrew in terms of his appearance.

Taylor Swift

Chris Pizzello/Invision/East News, Vianney Le Caer/Invision/East News, Evan Agostini/Invision/AP/East News, AUG/face to face/FaceToFace/REPORTER/East News

Amid the past relationships of Taylor Swift, a distinct pattern has emerged too. All of her past partners flaunt similarly shaded brown locks, styled in a carefree, swept-back manner. Whether it’s a fellow music sensation like Harry Styles, actors such as Jake Gyllenhaal, or even a member of American aristocracy like Conor Kennedy, those irresistible tousled hairdos that practically beckon for a touch remain consistently present.

Although we say some people in our article have a similar type, all of them are unique in their own way. But some may have even more unique singular features, taking the example of Harry Styles who surprisingly has 4 nipples.
