Late Bimbo Odukoyas son and wife mark 3rd wedding anniversary

Kemi marked their anniversary by posting a throwback photo of their wedding day on Instagram accompanying it with a lovely caption.

“3 years ago today. Time flies when you’re having fun! Very thankful for three fabulous years married to my best friend. I thank God always for our blessed marriage and I pray we have many more years together! Happy anniversary @iamthattipj #MyEternalBoyfriend”, she wrote.
