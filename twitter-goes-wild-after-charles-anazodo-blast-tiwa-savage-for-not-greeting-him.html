Twitter goes wild after Charles Anazodo blast Tiwa Savage for not greeting him

Okay, guys, the gist is that Charles Anazodo isn't happy with Tiwa Savage for walking past him without saying hello. The media personality and football analyst made this known via his Twitter page on Thursday, October 3, 2019.

"@TiwaSavage was a guest on @THEBEAT999FM earlier today, arrived and walked past @kallyjoe and me, and didn't have the courtesy to say hello. I guess being a superstar gives you the right to be discourteous," he tweeted.

It didn't take long before Tiwa Savage responded to his tweet which well...was more than everyone bargained for.

"First of all, I’m sure you saw me rushing to the studio because I was late for my interview plus I had a hat on, second I’m glad I didn’t say hi because who are you to think you can’t say hi first," she replied.

These tweets have since sent the Twitter community into a frenzy with people expressing their views. A lot of people think Charles was feeling entitled while for another camp, it was all about the hilarious angle to it.
