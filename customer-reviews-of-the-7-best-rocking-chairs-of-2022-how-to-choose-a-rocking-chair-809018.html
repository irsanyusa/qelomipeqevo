Customer Reviews of the 7 Best Rocking Chairs of 2022 | How to Choose a Rocking Chair

In the past, rocking chairs were designed for palliative care, for at least 2 people, for example, a mom and a baby or an elderly person and a nurse. But today, it seems like a “must” in a modern home. And we actually can’t object to that since it provides additional comfort, no matter what you plan to use it for.

However, choosing the right rocking chair could be challenging, and that’s why we at Bright Side have a few tips on how to find the right one. Plus, we have the best ones picked out from Amazon just for you.

5 things to look for in a rocking chair

No matter whether you are getting a rocking chair for nursing or just giving yourself a daily dose of relaxation time, there are a few factors that you need to consider before getting one.

  • Get a chair that doesn’t make a lot of noise. You don’t want to upset anyone around you, yourself included.
  • Choose the cushion color wisely. If you plan to use the rocking chair outside or you have kids and pets, it’s better to go for darker colors as it’s easier to hide stains.
  • Consider getting a rocking chair with covered hinges. This will provide you with better safety and prevents anything from getting stuck. If there aren’t any, get them separately here.
  • Buy a chair that is easy to use. And this is not just about sitting down and rocking, but also easy setup and simple angle and height adjustments.
  • Get one with supportive cushioning and a wide seat. This is especially essential if you plan to sit in the chair for longer periods, like when nursing. You need that extra comfort to move around. But if you need extra support and comfort, you can get this cushion.
