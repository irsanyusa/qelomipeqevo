An Inside Look at 'La La Land's' Boldest Musical Number

published on July 13, 2024
La La Land, the new movie musical with Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling, starts in gridlock. The scene: a freeway ramp on a blazingly sunny L.A. day. Cars glitter far into the horizon. Horns honk. And then the music picks up. That opening song-and-dance number, “Another Day of Sun,” is the film’s ode to the city of Los Angeles, a world of endless possibility, constant beauty—and repetitive disappointment.
