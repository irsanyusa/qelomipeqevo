Marc Anthony, 54, Married His 23-Year-Old Girlfriend and Proved That Love Has No Age Limit

Marc Anthony and Nadia Ferreira got married in a ceremony held in Miami, Florida. The wedding was a star-studded event attended by close family and friends. And we are here for our beloved readers to share all the info we know about the couple. And yes, this couple shows that love has no boundaries.

Love asks no questions, especially for this couple.

During a party in Miami, the pair announced their engagement just 3 months after making their romance official on Instagram. Less than a year after getting engaged, Marc Anthony and Nadia Ferreira wed on January 28, 2023. The 54-year-old Latin Grammy winner and Ferreira, 23, exchanged vows in a magnificent ceremony at Miami’s Perez Art Museum.

When it comes to weddings, they did their best.

The ceremony was attended by several prominent figures. As per reports, the bride, who once held the title of Miss Universe Paraguay, donned a stunning white Galia Lahav wedding dress with intricate lace and flower details. Meanwhile, the groom opted for a Christian Dior ensemble for the big day.

The couple’s loved ones attended their ceremony.

A number of famous faces were also present at the event, including the Beckhams (David and Cruz), Salma Hayek, Lin Manuel Miranda, Luis Fonsi, Daddy Yankee, Romeo Santos, Maluma, and Marco Antonio Solis, among others. While some may be curious about whether Jennifer Lopez made an appearance — it doesn’t look like she attended the ceremony.

Beckham and Carlos Slim served as Anthony’s best men. Slim’s daughter-in-law Maria Elena Torruco, who is married to Carlos Slim Domit, served as the bride’s maid of honor. The 2 children from Anthony’s previous marriage to Debbie Rosado, son Chase, 27, and daughter Ariana, 29, didn’t seem to be present at the wedding.

Marc still has a good relationship with his former partner, Jennifer Lopez.

When recalling Marc’s ties with Jennifer, we know they maintain a positive relationship. “Marc and I are good how we are right now,” Lopez said. “That has been even better for us. We met working, and that’s where we’re really magical, when we’re on stage together, and so we leave it there. That’s it.”

Congratulations to Marc Anthony and Nadia Ferreira on their marriage! It’s always exciting to hear about new love and commitment. Wishing them all the happiness and love in the world.
