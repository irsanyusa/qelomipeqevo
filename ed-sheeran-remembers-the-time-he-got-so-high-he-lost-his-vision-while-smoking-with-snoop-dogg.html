Ed Sheeran Remembers The Time He Got So High He Lost His Vision While Smoking With Snoop Dogg

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Arturo Holmes/WireImage, Photo by Phillip Faraone/Getty Images

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Ed Sheeran recently shared an amusing tale of an unexpected encounter with Snoop Dogg, that left him in quite a hazy situation. Although Sheeran isn’t famous for indulging in marijuana, the allure of sharing a smoke session with the iconic rapper was simply too intriguing.

Ed Sheeran Recalls a High Time with Snoop Dogg

In a chat with Conan O’Brien on the podcast Conan O’Brien Needs a Friend, Sheeran recounted the night he got “higher” than he ever expected. The story began with Sheeran and his wife, Cherry Seaborn, along with her mother, attending one of Snoop Dogg’s concerts in Melbourne, Australia. Following the show, they found themselves backstage, meeting the legendary rapper.

“I remember him meeting my mother-in-law, and he was like, ‘What’s up, queen?'” Sheeran said.

Sheeran fondly recalled, reflecting on the warm welcome Snoop extended to their group. It’s worth noting that Sheeran has developed a close friendship with actor Russell Crowe, who happens to share a tight bond with Snoop, often engaging in what Sheeran called “smoke-offs” and such.

While Sheeran confessed that he doesn’t typically partake in smoking weed, the atmosphere backstage that night was undeniably enticing. He described a scene where blunt after blunt was being passed around, and he felt a certain obligation to partake in the experience if only to say, “I smoked with Snoop Dogg.”

After a bit of wine and an engaging conversation with Snoop, the moment arrived. Snoop offered Sheeran some of the green stuff, and after a bit of hesitation, he decided to give it a try.

“I just remember looking at him and being like, ‘I can’t see right now,'” Sheeran hilariously recounted.

It was a feeling quite different from his norm, but Sheeran emphasized that he wouldn’t have expected anything less from the iconic rapper. After all, it wouldn’t be a true Snoop Dogg experience without getting a bit “higher” than usual.

Ed Sheeran Gets Drunk in New York City While Celebrating Album’s Release

Sheeran’s wild night in New York City didn’t stop with his memorable encounter with Snoop Dogg and Burna Boy. The singer, in a move that endeared him even more to his fans, embarked on an epic bar crawl across the Big Apple to celebrate the release of his latest album, Autumn Variations.

In a video shared on social media, Sheeran outlined his plans to hit random bars and buy rounds of drinks for strangers over a six-hour period. He greeted New Yorkers with a cheerful declaration that the drinks were on him for the night. As he made his way through the city, he chatted with fellow bar-goers, clinked glasses, and even posed for photos with staff at a Stout NYC location.

Throughout the night, Sheeran continued to spread the joy, taking the subway, playing beer pong with fans, and sharing shots with patrons at various bars. The singer’s infectious enthusiasm and generosity earned him the love and cheers of those he encountered along the way.
